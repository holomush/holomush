<objective>
Give operators a way to recover audit dead letters: a `holomush audit dlq
{list,show,replay}` cobra command that inspects the `EVENTS_AUDIT_DLQ` stream and
re-drives entries through the audit projection's write path back into `events_audit`
once the underlying outage (usually Postgres) is fixed.

Purpose: CLUSTER-04's recovery half — "dead letters you can't replay are just
nicer-looking data loss" (D-11). Capture (Plan 04) preserves the message; replay
restores it. Reusing the projection persist path keeps replay idempotent and
header-parsing-identical to the live path.
Output: `cmd/holomush/cmd_audit.go` + an exported audit replay entry + a real-broker
replay proof.
</objective>
