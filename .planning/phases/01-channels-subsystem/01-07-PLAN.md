The `aliases: ["="]` entry is the MED-6 `=` routing mechanism (seeded as a system prefix alias by the host manifest alias-seeder). The `{action: write, resource: channel}` capability is backed by the 01-04 `write-channel-as-member` policy (MED-5).
- `main.go`: route `channel` → `dispatchChannelCommand`; start the pruner goroutine at Init (with `prune_interval` config), stop on Unload.
