## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| audit projection ↔ Postgres | Persist failures (DB outage) are the primary cause of audit dead letters |
| DLQ stream (data at rest) | Captured messages carry event payloads (possibly sensitive/encrypted) |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-03-09 | Repudiation | audit dead letter silently dropped → audit-trail gap | high | mitigate | Capture-then-Term; Nak on DLQ-publish failure guarantees no drop (D-09); replay (Plan 07) restores to events_audit |
| T-03-10 | Denial of Service (disk exhaustion) | poison flood fills the DLQ stream | high | mitigate | Bounded MaxAge/MaxBytes retention (D-12); `holomush_audit_dlq_messages_total` alerts operators long before age-out (D-11) |
| T-03-11 | Info-disclosure | DLQ preserves headers/payload of sensitive events | medium | accept | Payloads are already the encrypted-at-rest envelope (per-field DEK); the DLQ stream inherits the same account scoping (`internal.>`, CLUSTER-02). No new plaintext surface |
| T-03-12 | Tampering | Nak-storm on ordinary poison path | medium | mitigate | Nak reserved for the DLQ-publish-failed fallback only; sub-cap path keeps AckWait backoff (Pitfall 3) |
</threat_model>
